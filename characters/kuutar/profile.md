## Features & Traits

### Racial Traits (Aasimar)
- **Darkvision:** 60 ft.
- **Celestial Resistance:** Resistance to necrotic and radiant damage
- **Healing Hands:** As an action, touch a creature and restore HP equal to your level (once per long rest)
- **Light Bearer:** Know the *Light* cantrip (CHA is spellcasting ability)
- **Celestial Revelation (Radiant Soul):** As a bonus action, gain a flying speed equal to walking speed for 1 minute (once per long rest)

### Class Features (Sorcerer)
- **Spellcasting:** CHA-based. Spell Save DC 14, Spell Attack Bonus +6
- **Font of Magic:** 4 sorcery points (regain all on long rest)
- **Flexible Casting:** Spend sorcery points to create spell slots (bonus action), or convert spell slots to sorcery points (bonus action)
- **Metamagic:** Twinned Spell (1 SP — target a second creature with a single-target spell), Quickened Spell (2 SP — change casting time from action to bonus action)
- **Subclass:** Astral Drifter (details TBD)

### Lunar Sorcery Features
- **Moonfire:** You know *Sacred Flame* (doesn't count against cantrips known). When you cast it, you can target one creature as normal or two creatures within range that are within 5 feet of each other.
- **Lunar Embodiment:** At each long rest, choose a moon phase (Full Moon, New Moon, or Crescent Moon). You can cast the associated 1st-level Lunar Spell once without expending a spell slot (once per long rest). Lunar spells don't count against spells known.
  - **Full Moon:** Shield (1st), Lesser Restoration (2nd)
  - **Crescent Moon:** Color Spray (1st), Alter Self (2nd)
  - **New Moon:** Ray of Sickness (1st), Blindness/Deafness (2nd)

## Personality

- **Traits:** Self-possessed and commanding — carries the gravity of a dethroned goddess. Trains others to the point of exhaustion because she sees iron will as the only true currency.
- **Ideals:** Strength is temporary; will is eternal. Being broken is a phase — the future belongs to those who push through it.
- **Bonds:** The wand she carries is all that remains of the Trilune Kingdom. It is both her proof of identity and the weight of a crown that no longer exists.
- **Flaws:** Trauma from the destruction of her kingdom caused significant memory loss. She cannot fully remember her past or find her way home. Her sense of divine self-importance can mask deep uncertainty.

## Notes

### Appearance
Aasimar — carries the silvered grace of the Lunar Blessing. Details TBD.

### Backstory
Born in the Astral Sea within the Trilune Kingdom, 6000+ years after the Funerary Flame (a war that destroyed her heritage). The true heir to the Trilune throne — but before she could rule, the kingdom was erased by war. She was forced to flee; her survival was deemed more precious than those who fell defending her. The trauma fractured her mind, leaving her with significant memory loss. She drifted through the Astral Sea as a wandering exile, forging bonds with distant deities and shaping others she met along the way. She knows she is the heir — her wand is proof — but she can no longer remember how to find home.
