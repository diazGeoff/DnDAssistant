# Session 4: Shadows on the Cairn Road

> **Date:** March 29, 2026
> **Location:** Red Larch → Cairn Road → Barren Hilltop (Shallow Graves)

---

## Summary

- Party discussing travel plans toward Beliard to investigate the missing diplomats
- **Lamond Greenboot** (farmer) entered using a cane, asked for the constable
- He spoke with Kaylessa; she approached the party with a new request — had a bad feeling about it, but it seemed to be on the way to Beliard
- **Lamond's report:** While tending his sheep, he discovered **shallow graves** in a patch of uninhabited valley — new, smelling of rot. A month ago, that area was clear.
- Party departed Red Larch, traveling the **Cairn Road** toward the shallow graves
- **Lamond Greenboot** led the way — casual, whistling, sheep following along
- Spotted **Feathergale Spire** — a tower visible from the road
- **Flying beasts** circling the top of Feathergale Spire
- Multiple **knights mounted on flying beasts** — more than you'd expect, an unusual number gathered at the spire
- Continued on to a **barren hilltop** — arrived at the shallow graves location
- Found **4 freshly dug graves** — stench of decay, vultures circling overhead
- **Excavated all 4 graves** — contents (Rytlock's investigation: the dwarf was being guarded by the female warrior — she was his escort):
  1. **Male dwarf** — artisan robes marked with the **heraldry of Mirabar**
  2. **Human female warrior** — carries a **red axe** bearing the **symbol of Mirabar**
  3. **Male human warrior** — dressed in **black robes**, wearing **stony armor** bearing the **triangle eye symbol** (earth cult)
  4. **Male human** — dressed in a **white robe decorated with black feathers** at the shoulders, bearing the **Auran symbol** (air cult / Howling Hatred)

- **Combat — Vulture Riders** attacked from above on giant vultures, swooping in with Ray of Cold from 40 ft. up
- **Note:** The attackers bear the **Auran symbol** matching body #4 — confirmed **Howling Hatred (air cult)**. Body #3 is earth cult (triangle symbol). Both factions were present at the scene of the diplomat's death.
- One vulture was killed by the Ranger — rider landed safely (used reaction to avoid prone)
- Frightened enemy with **Daunting Roar** — could not move toward the party
- Enemy attempted to flee — Rytlock landed an **opportunity attack** (greatsword), then fired a **crossbow hit (5 piercing)** — enemy still standing and running
- **Ranger** finished off the fleeing enemy
- Rest of the party killed the remaining attackers
- **1 attacker escaped** — they know the party's location and faces
- **Combat over**
- **Investigated the attacker bodies** — found the **Auran symbol** on them, confirming a connection to the **Howling Hatred** (air cult)
- The **Ranger** detected the Auran mark on one of the attackers — it traces back to **Feathergale Spire**. The spire is a confirmed Howling Hatred location.

- Party found a safer location away from the hilltop to take a **short rest** — area near the graves is too exposed
- **Lamond Greenboot** parted ways with the party during the rest — he returned to his sheep
- At **dawn**, the party spotted a **keep** in the distance (separate structure from Feathergale Spire)
- Party decided to head to **Feathergale Spire** — confirmed Howling Hatred base, likely holds answers about Rhundorth's missing document and the other diplomats
- Party arrived in the **Sighing Valley**
- Crossed a **stream** en route — the crossing was difficult enough to cause **Exhaustion (Level 1)** for the party
- **Ranger** used stealth to scout ahead — overheard **5 Aarakocra** (bird people) talking about not wanting their home taken by outsiders
- Ranger dropped stealth and approached — claimed to be lost (failed **Deception check**)
- Caught lying, the Ranger told the truth — the party is heading to the spire to take on the attackers
- One Aarakocra mentioned they had **seen more outsiders** recently
- Ranger described the attackers — the Aarakocra **confirmed the description matched** who they had seen in the valley
- Aarakocra shared their history with the air cult: **initially welcomed the settlers** into the Sighing Valley, but over time the cult **began meddling and trying to take over** their home
- The 5 Aarakocra introduced themselves:
  - **Kwal** — the leader
  - **Krook** — the grumpy one
  - **Kwikar** — female, barn owl
  - **Zeeles**
  - **Rharra**
- The Aarakocra **agreed to ally with the party** — they will intervene and help if the party is attacked from the air
- The Aarakocra briefed the party on the **Sighing Valley's dangers**: **gnolls**, **red blood hawks**, **griffons**, and a **manticore**
- The Aarakocra acted as **bait** to divert the air cult's attention — while they drew the cult away, the party moved under cover toward **Feathergale Spire**
- Party approached the **base of Feathergale Spire** — spotted a **bridge** above leading to the tower entrance
- **No air guards visible** — the Aarakocra's diversion drew the air cult away from the spire
- Party planning to scale the spire using a **grappling hook** to reach the bridge
- Spotted a **drawbridge (down) and locked gate** at the bridge level, and a **brass bell** used to announce visitors
- Found an **open door at the base of the tower** (below the bridge) — the Thief scouted it in stealth
- The lower entrance is a **stable** housing **vultures**, **griffons**, and **hippogriffs** — the air cult's full aerial cavalry
- A **secret door** inside the stable contains a **manticore**
- The Thief entered the stable door (invisible) and spotted a **guard feeding the manticore**
- **Kuutar** used **Alter Self (Natural Weapons)** — spent 1 2nd-level slot and 2 sorcery points (Quickened Spell)
- Party engaged the guard: Thief, Kuutar, and two Druids vs. one guard
- **Guard defeated** — party proceeds up the stairs into the spire
