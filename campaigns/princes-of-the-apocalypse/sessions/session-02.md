# Session 2: The Lord of Lance Rock

> **Date:** February 15, 2026
> **Location:** Lance Rock (Necromancer's Cave)

---

## Summary

- The entrance path collapsed, trapping the party inside Lance Rock
- Caught in a pincer attack — the 3 Dancing Dead (jester, lady, bear) in the star cavern and more undead from a room behind
- Defeated all enemies and pushed deeper into the cave
- Reached the necromancer's lair — found 3 stone slabs that appear to have bodies in/on them
- The necromancer revealed himself, calling himself **"The Lord of Lance Rock"**
- He had skeleton minions guarding him, but a stealthy teammate managed to close the distance
- The necromancer fled to a back room and summoned a **flesh golem** — immune to physical damage
- **Kuutar** (party mage) hit the golem with **fire magic**, dealing real damage
- The flesh golem, aggro'd by the fireball, charged toward Kuutar — away from the necromancer
- Some party members were pinned near the stone slabs by skeleton minions and the golem
- Split-attack strategy: 2 party members (including the stealthy one) broke through to attack the necromancer directly, while the rest held off the minions
- **The Lord of Lance Rock was killed** — the flesh golem collapsed when he died, and the remaining skeletons were mopped up
- **Loot:** The necromancer dropped a **wand** (currently held by the party druid — identity unknown, needs identifying)
- Found an **eye-shaped symbol/display** in the back room where the necromancer had fled
- Returned to **Red Larch** to rest
- Purchased a **cart and donkey**
- Went to the **shrine** to ask about the eye symbol from Lance Rock
- An **earthquake** hit Red Larch, opening a **sinkhole**
- **2 party members fell into the sinkhole**
- At the bottom: an **unknown area/map** — unexplored territory beneath Red Larch
