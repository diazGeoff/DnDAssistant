# Session 3

> **Date:** March 8, 2026
> **Location:** Red Larch — Sinkhole

---

## Summary

- The sinkhole is **40 feet deep**
- Many people fell in when it opened — there are **dead bodies** at the bottom
- Some people still **hanging on the edge**, others clinging to the **sides of the sinkhole**
- The party **ranger** (already at the bottom from falling in Session 2) helped the injured people down there
- **Town elders** standing at the sides of the sinkhole seem reluctant to help — appear to be hiding something
- **Bottom of the sinkhole:** flat bedrock floor with tree roots around the edges
  - **North:** a tunnel leading somewhere
  - **South:** a **stone slab door** — used robes and a waterskin found near it (signs someone has been here before)
- An old man gave **Kuutar** a coil of rope — his son (no more than 10 years old) is trapped in the sinkhole
- **Rescued everyone** from the sinkhole
- The wet floor near the stone slab door has dried out
- Opened the stone slab door — leads to a **slope down** into a pathway
- Inside: **maze-like pathways**
- Wall carvings depict **dwarves carrying battle axes**
- Reached another **stone slab door** — has dwarf carvings with **skid marks** and **10x10 tile gaps** between each carving
- **Kuutar** and the **Land Druid** fell into the tile gaps — trapped inside by smooth stone that sealed over them (chain/counterweight mechanism, perfectly sealed)
- Kuutar used **Acid Splash** to chip a small hole in the slab, then **Misty Step** to escape
- The Land Druid also escaped
- Returned to the stone slab door — there's a **small opening** (not the full slab) with an **eye looking straight through it** at the party
- The small opening closed, then the full **slab door opened** — reveals a **large square chamber**
  - **West side:** a door
  - The eye belonged to a **half-orc** on the other side
  - **Center:** a stone table with a **person pinned down by stones on all limbs**
- **Combat:** Kuutar attacked the half-orc — **downed** with Eldritch Blast
  - Half-orc used **Relentless Endurance**, got back up and **grappled Kuutar**
  - Kuutar tried **Persuasion** — the half-orc is **simple-minded** and was **punished by the elders** (not a willing participant)
- Moved on and found a **human corpse** being eaten by **5 giant rats**
- The corpse has the **eye symbol carved into it** — same symbol found in the necromancer's lair at Lance Rock
- Passed the giant rats without engaging
- At the end of the path: a **floating black rock**
  - Detect Magic reveals **transmutation magic**
  - Has a **cylindrical hole on top** — something is meant to be inserted
  - Thief used **Mage Hand** to pull the floating rock — once removed from the cylindrical magic source, the rock became heavy (no longer floating)
  - Removing the rock **opened a door**
- Behind the door: a **newly dug path** — noticeably not dwarven construction (unlike the carved pathways before)
- Reached a **door with gaps** — a **light source** visible on the other side
- **Rytlock** opened the door — reveals what appears to be an **office**
  - Dwarven construction with **books** inside
  - The newly dug path appears to be a **shortcut** to this room (bypassing the original dwarven routes)
  - Investigated the books — contain **reports** spanning from **~1 year ago** to as recent as **3 days ago**
  - The reports track **people passing through Red Larch** — descriptions (not names), including a **report describing the party**
  - Reports are written/signed in **Terran script** (the language of earth elementals)
  - The report about the party says: **"Kaylessa hires adventurers — stop activities"**
  - All reports stamped with the **eye symbol** (triangle with eye) — same as Lance Rock and the corpse
  - Also found **3 other symbols** on some reports — written in **Primordial** (the base language of elemental tongues)
  - One report in **Auran** (read by the Ranger): a message from the **Howling Hatred** — threatening displeasure at skirmishes against them and promising vengeance
