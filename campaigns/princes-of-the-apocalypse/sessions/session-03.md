# Session 3

> **Date:** March 8, 2026
> **Location:** Red Larch — Sinkhole

---

## Summary

- The sinkhole is **40 feet deep**
- Many people fell in when it opened — there are **dead bodies** at the bottom
- Some people still **hanging on the edge**, others clinging to the **sides of the sinkhole**
- The party **ranger** (already at the bottom from falling in Session 2) helped the injured people down there
- **Town elders** standing at the sides of the sinkhole seem reluctant to help — appear to be hiding something
- **Bottom of the sinkhole:** flat bedrock floor with tree roots around the edges
  - **North:** a tunnel leading somewhere
  - **South:** a **stone slab door** — used robes and a waterskin found near it (signs someone has been here before)
- An old man gave **Kuutar** a coil of rope — his son (no more than 10 years old) is trapped in the sinkhole
- **Rescued everyone** from the sinkhole
- The wet floor near the stone slab door has dried out
- Opened the stone slab door — leads to a **slope down** into a pathway
- Inside: **maze-like pathways**
- Wall carvings depict **dwarves carrying battle axes**
- Reached another **stone slab door** — has dwarf carvings with **skid marks** and **10x10 tile gaps** between each carving
- **Kuutar** and the **Land Druid** fell into the tile gaps — trapped inside by smooth stone that sealed over them (chain/counterweight mechanism, perfectly sealed)
- Kuutar used **Acid Splash** to chip a small hole in the slab, then **Misty Step** to escape
- The Land Druid also escaped
- Returned to the stone slab door — there's a **small opening** (not the full slab) with an **eye looking straight through it** at the party
- The small opening closed, then the full **slab door opened** — reveals a **large square chamber**
  - **West side:** a door
  - The eye belonged to a **half-orc named Grund** on the other side
  - **Center:** a stone table with a **person pinned down by stones on all limbs**
- **Combat:** Kuutar attacked the half-orc — **downed** with Eldritch Blast
  - Half-orc used **Relentless Endurance**, got back up and **grappled Kuutar**
  - Kuutar tried **Persuasion** — the half-orc is **simple-minded** and was **punished by the elders** (not a willing participant)
  - Half-orc still fights (coerced) — Kuutar hit with **Mind Sliver**
  - Kuutar escaped grapple (Acrobatics), half-orc chased
  - **Land Druid** entered the chamber while orc was distracted — the person pinned to the stone table is an **11-year-old boy** (not the old man's son — different kid)
  - Half-orc threw Kuutar outside the chamber (no damage)
  - Land Druid **freed the boy** — the boy is starving, **begging for food**
  - The boy has been **punished for 3 days** — he failed to deliver a letter from his father **Rotarharterhand** to **Ilmeth Waelver** (a wagon seller in Red Larch)
  - Kuutar **flew out of the sinkhole** (Celestial Revelation — Acrobatics success)
  - Land Druid used **DM Inspiration** to try to hide the boy — **failed**
  - Half-orc spotted the boy and **dragged him back**
- Moved on and found a **human corpse** being eaten by **5 giant rats**
- The corpse has the **eye symbol carved into it** — same symbol found in the necromancer's lair at Lance Rock
- Passed the giant rats without engaging
- At the end of the path: a **floating black rock**
  - Detect Magic reveals **transmutation magic**
  - Has a **cylindrical hole on top** — something is meant to be inserted
  - Thief used **Mage Hand** to pull the floating rock — once removed from the cylindrical magic source, the rock became heavy (no longer floating)
  - Removing the rock **opened a door**
- Behind the door: a **newly dug path** — noticeably not dwarven construction (unlike the carved pathways before)
- Reached a **door with gaps** — a **light source** visible on the other side
- **Rytlock** opened the door — reveals what appears to be an **office**
  - Dwarven construction with **books** inside
  - The newly dug path appears to be a **shortcut** to this room (bypassing the original dwarven routes)
  - Investigated the books — contain **reports** spanning from **~1 year ago** to as recent as **3 days ago**
  - The reports track **people passing through Red Larch** — descriptions (not names), including a **report describing the party**
  - Reports are written/signed in **Terran script** (the language of earth elementals)
  - The report about the party says: **"Kaylessa hires adventurers — stop activities"**
  - All reports stamped with the **eye symbol** (triangle with eye) — same as Lance Rock and the corpse
  - Also found **3 other symbols** on some reports — written in **Primordial** (the base language of elemental tongues)
  - One report in **Auran** (read by the Ranger): a message from the **Howling Hatred** — threatening displeasure at skirmishes against them and promising vengeance
  - **Thief** secretly pocketed a **report as evidence**
- Rytlock opened a **dwarven door** on the opposite side of the office (opened easily)
  - Room beyond has a **broken dwarven statue** in the center, propped up with supports (has shield and axe)
  - Inscription reads: **"Petrified [???] dwarf found in [date]"** — the statue is not a carving, it's a **petrified dwarf**
  - In front of the statue: **coins, gems, and a blood-stained dagger** — offerings/shrine
  - **Dream Druid** checked with **Arcana** — the dwarf was petrified by an **ailment** (disease/curse), not a creature
  - **Thief** used **Mage Hand** to collect the **coins, gems, and bloody dagger** from the shrine
  - The dagger's pommel is inscribed with **"Reszur"** — possibly the weapon's name
- **Kuutar** (at the surface) spots **6 cloaked figures** moving from the tunnel toward the entrance of the dwarven dungeon
  - Their armor bears the **triangle eye symbol** — confirmed **earth cult members**
- The **half-orc** tried to rescue the **Land Druid** and bring them toward the chamber while being chased by the **6 cultists**
- The **Land Druid** triggered the **slab traps** — **2 cultists got through**, but **4 got trapped**
- The **Land Druid** engaged in combat with the **2 remaining cultists**
- **Battle ends** — one cultist taken down by **Acid Splash**, the **half-orc knocked the other unconscious**
- **Rytlock** and the rest of the party investigated the **other room** connected to the battle chamber
- **Tied up the unconscious cultist** — **Rytlock** is carrying them until they wake up for interrogation
- The **boy** explained the **Believers**:
  - When he and his father became Believers, they were shown a **room with moving stones**
  - The Believers fear that **something bad will happen if the stones move** — punishments are meant to appease whatever controls the stones
- The party **persuaded Grund** to replace the boy with the **unconscious cultist** on the punishment table — Grund still felt obligated to punish someone, so the swap satisfied him
- **Kuutar** rejoined the party underground
- Opened the door behind the petrified dwarf room — **zigzag passages** leading to a dead end
  - At the end: a **lantern** beside an **old man** who **begged for mercy** upon seeing the party
  - The old man warns that behind the door are the **moving stones** — and if the party **disturbs the Delvers**, it will cause **disaster**
  - The **Thief** peeked through the door — the old man warns **not to move the stones**
- The party **deceived the old man** into believing they are Believers and **not associated with Kaylessa**
- Party entered the **moving stones room**:
  - **Center:** stone **monoliths of various shapes** — these are the **Delvers** themselves (the stones are living things)
  - The Delvers can **predict anything that happens in Red Larch**
  - Dwarven dwellings already existed throughout the valley, but the **Delvers were only discovered years ago**
  - The **Believers** sent for an **earth priest** to understand and **pacify** the Delvers
  - **Against the walls:** **6 stone slabs** containing **bones**
  - **Investigation check** revealed a **hidden passage** blended into the stone
  - **Ranger** opened the secret door — blocked with **canvas and loose stone**, very **dusty** (hasn't been used in a long time)
