# Session 1

> **Date:** —
> **Location:** Red Larch / Haunted Tombs / Lance Rock

---

## Summary

- Received a letter from **Kaylessa** (innkeeper) asking the party to investigate **four shadowy figures**
- Arrived at **Red Larch** and set up at **The Swinging Sword** inn
- Ventured out to the **Haunted Tombs** outside town
- Encountered a **Ghost** guarding the tomb
- Found a table inside with **3 rusty figures** that appear to indicate items once placed there:
  - A **circle** shape
  - A **long** shape
  - A **box** shape
- Traveled to **Lance Rock**
- Encountered an undead at the entrance — killed it by burning
- Detected magic coming from a **necromancer** deeper inside
- Reached the **star-shaped cavern** (L4 — Dancing Dead) and were attacked by **3 unique undead**:
  - A **jester** with jingling bells
  - A **fair lady** in frills
  - A **bear-like** costumed zombie

## Open Questions

- What are the three items the rusty figures represent? Where are they now?
- What is the ghost's story? Why is it guarding the tomb?
- Who are the four shadowy figures Kaylessa mentioned?
- Who is the necromancer at Lance Rock?
- What lies deeper in the Lance Rock caves?
