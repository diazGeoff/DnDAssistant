# Session 5

> **Date:** April 19, 2026
> **Location:** Feathergale Spire (interior)

---

## Summary

- Party entered Feathergale Spire through the ground-floor stable after the Aarakocra created a diversion
- Defeated a guard feeding the manticore in the stable
- Heading up the stairs into the spire
- Reached a floor with **3 rooms**:
  1. One room **smells like petrichor** (rain/ozone scent)
  2. One room is **silent** — no sound, no smell
  3. One room **smells good — looks like a kitchen**
- Tried the **silent room** first — it's a **sleeping quarters / dormitory**
- Party split to investigate:
  - **Rytlock** guarded the dormitory door while the **Thief** went inside to inspect and search the room
  - **Kuutar** and the **Ranger** slowly opened the **petrichor room** — well-lit interior with **plants** growing inside
  - **2 guards** inside the petrichor room
  - **Theory:** The plants may be used to brew **concoctions** (potions, poisons, or alchemical mixtures)
- **Kitchen** has **4 guards** inside
- Checked the **main door** (coming up from the basement) — quickly spotted **3 people** on the other side, but couldn't get a clear look before closing the door
- **Successfully stealthed** — none of the 9 hostiles on this floor have detected the party
- **Floor total:** 2 guards (plant room) + 4 guards (kitchen) + 3 people (main door) = **9 hostiles**
- Party decided to **bypass the floor** and head up to the **next floor**
- Found an **ornate door** on the upper floor
- Listening at the door — hear **metal-on-metal shaking** and **metal-on-wood scraping** from inside
- Also hear **2 people talking** behind the ornate door
- Party decided to **skip the ornate door** and go up another floor
- New floor: **4 doors** around the room and a **trap door** above leading further up
- **Trap door** — sunlight visible from behind it
- Pushed it open — **sky and grass** visible above. The trap door leads to the **rooftop** of Feathergale Spire
- The **4 rooms** on this floor are all **silent** — nothing audible from any of them
- **1 of the 4 rooms** stands out — noticeably **cleaner and more well-appointed** than the rooms on the first floor. Likely an **officer's or leader's quarters**
- Investigated the nicer room — contains **2 beds**, **2 chests**, and a **tapestry depicting 2 winged beasts**
- Found **books on a table** — contain **reports and observation logs** (surveillance records of what they've seen)
- The room's occupant is affiliated with the **Feathergale Society** — the air cult's organization at the spire
- Chests contain **valuables** and a **black feathered robe** — matches the robes worn by the vulture rider attackers from Session 4 and the air cult body in the shallow graves
- **Rytlock** investigated another room — found **valuables** and a **rapier**. Told **Kuutar** to take the rapier
- Checked another room — found an **unfinished letter** written by the room's occupant
- **All rooms contain robes** — the entire party **donned the black feathered robes** as disguises to blend in with the Feathergale Society
- Opened the **trap door** and reached the **rooftop** — covered in **artificial grass lawn**
- Rooftop has **white stones** arranged like a **compass / directional marker** and **spyglasses** adjacent to it — an **observation post** for surveilling the valley
- As the **Thief** climbed onto the rooftop, a **person already up there** confronted her — suspicious of the elf, asked *"What are you doing here?"*
- The person appears to be a **high-ranking official** of the Feathergale Society
- He seems to **know who the party is** — likely informed by the escaped vulture rider from Session 4
- He is **surprised and appalled** by the party's presence — asks what their purpose is
- The party challenged him about **stealing the tower from the Aarakocra**
- He claims the tower was **unoccupied** when the Feathergale Society arrived
- He is **furious** that the party dug up the graves — accuses them of desecrating the site
- He claims the **air cult did not bury the bodies** — denies responsibility for the graves
- Party pointed out that vulture riders attacked them at the graves
- He explains the riders were **looking for their missing members** — saw the party at the graves and assumed the worst
- The air cult **lost one of their own** (body #4 — white robe with black feathers) at the grave site
- The official identified himself as **Thurl Merosska** — **Lord Commander of the Feathergale Society**, former **Waterdeep griffon rider**
- Party resolved the misunderstanding — **removed the stolen robes** and dropped the disguise
- Asked Thurl about the Feathergale Society's conflict with the **Aarakocra**
- Party and Thurl **settled their animosity** — both sides share a **common enemy in the earth cult**. Tentative alliance formed on the rooftop.
- Thurl directed the party to the earth cult's base: **Sacred Stone Monastery**
- Party asked about the **Aarakocra conflict** — the party is also allied with Kwal's group
- Thurl blames the Aarakocra for the tension; suggests the party can **pacify the Aarakocra by helping cull the manticores** in the valley
- **Note:** This contradicts Kwal's account — the Aarakocra said the air cult began "trying to take over" their valley after being welcomed in
- Party struck a **deal with Thurl**: help kill the **manticores** in the valley (to pacify the Aarakocra), and the party gets **lodging in the tower**
- Party plans to **mediate between Kwal and Thurl** — they're the only group both sides are willing to talk to
  - Strategy: be honest with Kwal about meeting Thurl, lead with the manticore cull as common ground, don't vouch for Thurl, and ask Kwal what coexistence actually requires
  - Goal: improve relations across the Sighing Valley ecosystem — benefit both the Aarakocra and the Feathergale Society
- **Kuutar** used the rooftop **spyglass** and spotted a **hooded figure** entering a **concealed entrance** somewhere in the valley
- Party took a **long rest** in the tower — Thurl honored the lodging deal
- After the long rest, **Kuutar** chose **Crescent Moon** for Lunar Embodiment (Color Spray 1st, Alter Self 2nd)

- Party preparing to mount up and ride out to the **manticore lair** for the cull
- Mounted **4 hippogriffs** from the spire's stable and took to the air
- While riding toward the manticore lair, **intercepted by 1 manticore** mid-air — combat in the sky
- Engaged the manticore in **aerial combat** on hippogriff-back
- **Killed the first manticore**
- Flew to the **manticore lair** — found it **empty**
- Returned to the first manticore's carcass — found a **second manticore** feeding on it
- Engaged the **second manticore** in combat — caught while distracted and feeding
- **Killed the second manticore**
- **Kuutar looted 12 manticore tail spikes** — converted for use as crossbow bolts with **poison damage**
- **Rytlock, Kuutar, and 2 others** returned to **Feathergale Spire** to report the cull to Thurl
- **Land Druid and Ranger** stayed at the carcass site to **inform the Aarakocra** about the dead manticores
- Thurl rewarded the party for completing the manticore cull:
  - **5 PP**
  - **17 GP**
  - **Ring with the Emblem of the Waterdeep Griffon Rider** (Thurl's former order)
- Reward loot held by the **Dream Druid** pending currency conversion and party split
- **Rytlock, Kuutar, and the Thief** were invited to a **feast in the spire** by Thurl — session ended here
